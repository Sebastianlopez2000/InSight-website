About Us

Welcome to InSight, previously known as Small More Marketing. We have transformed digitally to offer new and improved services. Our mission is to provide data-driven growth strategies. We are dedicated to helping small startups with limited marketing resources achieve their entrepreneurial dreams.

Introducing the InSight App

Our app helps you understand your market position and identify your closest competitors. Just paste your website URL into the app, and it will extract relevant information using a customized API and AI technology.

The app then creates a personalized marketing audit for your company, highlighting your strengths and areas for improvement. This audit can be downloaded as a PDF on your iPhone or MacBook.

Based on the audit, the app suggests the top 3 marketing campaigns that best suit your needs. Learn about each campaign's strategy, requirements, expected ROI, and average costs.

If a campaign interests you, click the 'Visualize' button to see a 3D interactive preview on your device. For example, visualize a billboard ad for your campaign in Piccadilly Circus. You can also use a VR headset for an immersive experience.

If you want to refine the campaign design or make the VR preview a reality, contact us to schedule a meeting.
