Overview

Welcome to our Marketing App! Enhance your marketing strategies with our cutting-edge technology and AI integration. Simply paste your website URL into the app to unlock a comprehensive marketing audit and gain insights into your market positioning and closest competitors.

Features

  • Website Analysis & Information Extraction

    Paste your website URL into the app and let our customized API extract all relevant information. Our AI analyzes this data to determine your market position and identify your closest competitors.

  • Bespoke Marketing Audit

    Receive a detailed marketing audit outlining your company's current marketing efforts. Understand strengths and areas for improvement. View and download the audit as a PDF on your iPhone and MacBook.

  • Campaign Recommendations

    Get top 3 recommended marketing campaigns tailored to your needs. Learn about each campaign's strategy, requirements, ROI, and associated costs.

Interactive 3D Visualizations

Visualize suggested campaigns in a 3D interactive environment. For example, see a billboard ad for your campaign in Piccadilly Circus. Use VR headsets for an immersive experience.
